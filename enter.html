Iggy’s Mowing Service


Coming from a household in which I was always pushed to be hardworking, I adopted the mindset at a young age that I must work to be financially independent. While there are many homeowners in the area where I live, yard maintenance isn’t something that everyone has time for or is able to do. Growing up, I would often assist neighbors with outdoor activities. Buying my first riding lawn mower at the age of fourteen, I was able to begin maintaining neighbors’ lawns;I quickly realized that people were looking for lawn care services outside of mowing, which I could provide. Using the previous year’s revenue, I purchased professional grade equipment at age sixteen and expanded my business to many different clients around the local area. Maintaining this professional business has provided me the opportunity to further my understanding of what it takes to be successful. Running this business has taught me how critical it is to have skills such as time management, an ability to engage in profit-based analysis, communication, and close record keeping. After two to three years of this business, I can proudly say that I have learned from past mistakes and improved upon my business practices, allowing me to be successful in this business today. I look forward to the opportunity to apply the professional skills I have built to new workplace situations. Spring of 2014- Present

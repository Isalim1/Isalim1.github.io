Iggy’s Mowing Service


Coming from a household in which I was always pushed to be hardworking, I was told that I must seek ways to make my own profits. I found that outside home care was something that not everyone had time for or was able to do. Starting off small, I would assist neighbors with outdoor activities. Buying my first riding lawn mower at the age of 14, I was able to mow nearby lawns. Realizing that this was something I can really expand, I purchased professional grade equipment at 16 and expanded my business to many different clients around the local area. Maintaining this professional business I have exceeded my understanding of what it takes to be successful. Running this business I have learned how critically effective it is to have skills such as Time management, profit based analysis, communication, and the tracking of records. Starting off I can proudly say that I have made mistakes that I have overcome and improved upon allowing me to be successful in this business today.  Spring of 2014- Present
